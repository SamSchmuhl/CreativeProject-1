GARETHAK

- The Stone Sword -

An ancient weapon of bygone age, this sword has been passed down through the lineage of the Grae Kings in Aedmari. Never has the holder of Garethak been defeated in battle, though wielding it takes a heavy toll upon the bearer. With the usurper Rasaty Drothar claiming herself the Queen of the Clans, the King of Clan Grae may have need of Garethak soon enough.
